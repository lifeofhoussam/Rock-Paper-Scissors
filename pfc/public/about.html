Rock Paper Scissors - About

Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors, a multiplayer game developed by LOUKILI Houssam as part of a university project.

This game was built using JavaScript, Node.js and Socket.io.

In this classic game, players select either rock, paper, or scissors to compete against each other.

The game offers a simple yet engaging gameplay that is perfect for players of all ages.

Whether you're looking for a quick distraction or a fun way to pass the time with friends, Rock Paper Scissors is the perfect choice.

I hope you enjoy playing this game as much as I enjoyed creating it. Have fun playing Rock Paper Scissors!
