Welcome to Rock Paper Scissors!

Rock-Paper-Scissors is a two-player game where each player selects one of three options: rock, paper, or scissors.

The objective of the game is to defeat your opponent by selecting a gesture that beats their gesture.

Rock beats scissors, as rock crushes scissors, while scissors beats paper, as it cuts paper.

On the other hand, paper beats rock, as it covers rock.

If both players choose the same option, the result is a tie.

To determine a winner in a single round, both players must make their choice, without knowing the other player's choice.

Use the toolbar above to play the game!
